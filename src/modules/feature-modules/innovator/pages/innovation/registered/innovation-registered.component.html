Your next steps

  • Answer questions in each section of your innovation record.
  • Mark each section as complete.
  • Submit your innovation record for needs assessment.
  • Select your data sharing preferences for support organisations.

You can invite your colleagues to collaborate on your innovation. Add collaborators in your innovation settings (opens in a new window).

When you submit your innovation record

The needs assessment team will:

  • review your innovation record to assess your needs.
  • notify the appropriate support organisations.
  • contact you with next steps.

This process usually takes 1 week.
