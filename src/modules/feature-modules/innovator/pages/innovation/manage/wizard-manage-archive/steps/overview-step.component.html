You can continue to edit and update your innovation record when it is archived. You cannot access support during this time. If you want support on your innovation in future, you can submit your record for a needs reassessment.

When you archive an innovation:

  • all support on your innovation will end
  • all open tasks will be cancelled
    •
  • you cannot send or reply to messages
  • you can view your previous tasks, messages and documents
  • previous organisations that have supported you will see an archived copy of your innovation
  • you and your collaborators can continue to edit and update your innovation record
  • your updates cannot be viewed by your previous support organisations
