You need to request permission from the innovator to download and share their innovation record in PDF format.

The innovator will be notified of your request and can approve or reject it. If the innovator approves, everyone from your organisation unit will be able to export the innovation record for the next 30 days.

Information:

You can use the data for the purpose of:

  • providing the health and social care system (or parts of the health and social care system) in the UK with information on Health Technologies (such as guidance, advice, and policies)
  • commissioning
  • supporting the development or adoption of Health Technologies
  • for medical/health technology procurement
  • continuous improvement of the NHS Innovation Service and Support Organisation processes
    and/or
  • such other purposes as may be agreed in writing between the Support Organisation and a Company from time to time

For more information, please see the terms of use (opens in a new window).

Let the innovator know how their information will be used, the people or organisations it will be shared with, and the reason for the request.
